# Response Quality Rubric

Evaluate the AI response based on these criteria:

## Criteria

### 1. Correctness (50 points)
- Response directly answers the question asked (30 pts)
- Information provided is accurate (20 pts)

### 2. Clarity (30 points)
- Response is clear and understandable (15 pts)
- Response is appropriately concise (15 pts)

### 3. Helpfulness (20 points)
- Response demonstrates understanding of the question (10 pts)
- Response provides useful information (10 pts)

## Scoring Guide
- 90-100: Excellent - Perfect response, clear and accurate
- 70-89: Good - Minor issues but overall correct and helpful
- 50-69: Acceptable - Some issues but fundamentally correct
- 0-49: Failing - Major issues, incorrect or unhelpful

## Pass Threshold
Score >= 70 to pass
